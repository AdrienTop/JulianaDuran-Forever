I have questions!

Where are we heading to?

For our guests traveling from afar: Carmen de Apicalá is a little town about 130 km south of Bogotá. With temperatures hovering around 29°C year-round, it's one of Colombia's favourite spots to do absolutely nothing productive. The venue, Conjunto El Imperio, is a countryside estate nestled among mountains and lush greenery. Dress accordingly! (yes, that means swimsuit and sunscreen).

How do i get there?

If you're driving from Bogotá, it's a straightforward route: Bogotá → Girardot → Melgar → Carmen de Apicalá. No car? No problem. Once you confirm, we'll sort out a shuttle leaving from Bogotá for those who need it. We're not leaving anyone behind!

Where can I stay?

El Imperio has hundreds of rental villa to chose from, right on-site. Once you RSVP, we’ll happily help you find an accommodation within walking distance of the party. We want hang out with you all weekend. Having everyone in the same place is our dream. We chose this location because Juli’s family has a special connection with this place, her family has had summer houses there for a long time and spend most of their weekends there. It's kind of the Duran village.

What do I wear?

Dress code: Tropical chic, make sure its breathable. We're outdoors, it's hot, it's humid, and we want you looking fabulous (not melting). Our recommendation: Linen, cotton or any light weight fabrics. Honestly, the default outfit around here is a swimsuit, but it's a wedding so we had to draw the line somewhere. You wont need a jacket.
